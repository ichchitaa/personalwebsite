My teaching experiences in college

Academic Intern for CS 70

I was an Academic Intern and member of course staff for CS 70 (a discrete maths and probability theory class at UC Berkeley) in Spring 2022. As part of this role, I assisted students in office hours with conceptual understanding of course material, as well as helping with problem-solving methodology and challenging homework problems.

CS 70 Mentor in Computer Science Mentors (CSM)

In Spring 2022, I led a group of 6 students in biweekly small-group tutoring sessions as part of the student organizaton Computer Science Mentors. For this role, I delivered mini-lectures and walked students through harder problems and exam questions. At the end of the semester, I got an average rating of 4.2/5 for the value of my sections and an average rating of 4.8/5 for the level of comfort students felt in my sections.

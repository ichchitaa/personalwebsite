About Me

Hi there! Thanks for stumbling on my little corner of the internet! My name is Ichchitaa (pronounced Ich-i-ta). I’m 20 years old and am a rising junior at UC Berkeley majoring in Computer Science and Economics!

My family is from India but I grew up mostly in Oman (and also lived in Bahrain for two years) going to international schools. As a result, I’m a bit of a nomad but this gave me a strong appreciation for different cultures and I love learning as much as I can about the world around me!

Outside of academics, my hobbies include reading, thrill activities, water sports and trying food places. I also love traveling - I have visited 64 countries and all 7 continents!

In this website you will find some of my personal project work, here for your perusal. As well as this, I’ve included writings about my travels, thrill activities and other cool things!

Feel free to get in touch about anything! I would love to talk to you :)
